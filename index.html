CLICKS FOR FICS

The Archive of Our Own platform hosts 6.9 million users who share their enthusiasm for fan fiction, resulting in nearly 13 million works. These works expand fictional realms with stories, relationships, and even new characters.

This project analyzes the most popular works based on Kudos (similar to likes), including the top 1000 works overall and the top 1000 works for each of Marvel, Harry Potter, and Boku No Hero Academia

The analysis provides insights into the themes and characters that resonate most with fans. Interactive data visualizations showcase the diversity and creativity of fan fiction.

Explore Relationships to see character interactions, and Additional Tags to discover common themes and tags. Have fun exploring!

Explore the Themes and Ratings of Fan Works

AI-driven analysis categorizes all the tags used in the top-rated works into 16 themes. The ratings, ranging from Explicit, Mature, Teen And Up Audiences, General Audiences, to Not Rated, are represented in the background colors to give a sense of the overall mood and content of the fandoms.

Explore the thematic overviews for Overall, Marvel, Harry Potter, and Boku No Hero Academia to gain deeper insights into fan content. Alongside the thematic overview, the actual tags can be explored to gain a better understanding of what content fans are reading.
